The workshop

Where everything is designed, made, finished.

I set up my studio at Chabreloche and if you pass by do not hesitate to come and discover the hidden face of the manufacture of an art knife. You will see the machines and tools that allow you to move from a piece of wood or horn, and a piece of metal, to a finished knife. Lathe, milling machine, oven, drills, files, hammer, rivets... And so many other essential accessories for my work.
