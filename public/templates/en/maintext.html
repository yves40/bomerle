TThe art of Cutlery

Welcome to the lair of Beau Merle, Bastien Toubhans, known as Bastos. Arriving late in the art cutlery business after long studies as a metallurgical engineer, I set up my workshop in Arconsat in Auvergne to work with my hands. Here I present my creations to you in the form of a themed gallery. Strongly inspired by the fine cutlery of the 18th and 19th centuries, French and English, my knives are chimeras. A mix of styles, eras with a touch of modernity. All my production is intended for the table, sharing a good time around a meal and the knife tool. I particularly like working around materials, mechanics and knife embellishment techniques bordering on goldsmithing. In my workshop, I make luxury pocket knives, entirely from the first sketch to the latest finishes starting from fine cutlery steel and raw materials animals or wood. I wish you a good visit.
